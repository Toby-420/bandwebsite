Why?

As we are very new to the music scene - relatively to others - we do not get any money from this. We are very much playing for a song.
Anything donated here is guaranteed to go directly into the band account for spending on equipment such as microphones, PAs, and strings.
This will probably cease to exist if we start to get any serious amount of income which would allow us to continue without donations.

Where?

Shortly, we will probably be setting up a joint account for the band in some form. We will allow transfers to this.
