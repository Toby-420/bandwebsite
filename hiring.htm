What you can hire us for

The usual events such as parties, weddings (yes we can play Uptown funk as Toby has a 5 string bass), and gigs at pubs can be done.
Just give us the set list if you have one. If not, tell us what sort of music you would like and we will find something.

If you have a question or are thinking of asking us to play, contact our email
