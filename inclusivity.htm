What this means

We take every step we can to make sure that everyone can experience our content as best they can
This does not mean that we do this better than anyone else, but we try our best

What this includes

  • Not retuning songs in the studio
  • Producing official tabs/sheet music
  • Diversity and Equality
  • Web accessibility

Retuning songs

When a song is retuned, you have to retune your instrument slightly to make it sound correct
This can be irritating as it is usually a small change that cannot be done by ear
This does not include tuning the instrument themselves down to a different tuning to reach lower notes

Official tabs/sheet music

We will produce official ones written by the player themselves to make sure they are as accurate as possible
They will also be free
This does not stop other people from making their own tabs/arrangements - please go ahead!

Diversity

We actively support people of all backgrounds, identities, and beliefs through our music and actions

Accessibility

This website was made within strict guidelines of accessibility standards
This means it is fully navigable by only keyboard, only mouse, usable by screen reader, and eyetracker
It also uses the least Javascript it needs to reduce loading times
